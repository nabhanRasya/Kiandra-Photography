Our Journey

Founded in 2015, Kiandra Photography began as a passion project in the heart of Bogor. What started as a small studio has grown into a team of dedicated photographers and creatives who share a common vision: to capture the authentic emotions and unique stories of each couple we work with.

Our journey has taken us across Indonesia and beyond, documenting love stories in various cultural settings. From traditional Javanese weddings to modern international celebrations, we've had the privilege of being part of countless special moments.
